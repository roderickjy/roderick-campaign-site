Hamilton’s Debt & Who We Owe

Hamilton City debt is ~NZ$1.2–1.3B. Major channels include the LGFA (on-lending from bond markets), some direct bank facilities, and institutional investors who ultimately hold the paper. We publish it, then we shrink it. [oai_citation:11‡roderickjy.github.io](https://roderickjy.github.io/roderick-campaign-site/)

  • LGFA on-lends to councils; councils collectively guarantee each other’s debt. [oai_citation:12‡roderickjy.github.io](https://roderickjy.github.io/roderick-campaign-site/)
  • Historic bank loans may remain for cash-flow; LGFA usually cheaper/longer-dated. [oai_citation:13‡roderickjy.github.io](https://roderickjy.github.io/roderick-campaign-site/)
  • Bondholders: NZ Super, KiwiSaver funds, insurers, offshore pension/sovereign funds. [oai_citation:14‡roderickjy.github.io](https://roderickjy.github.io/roderick-campaign-site/)

We’ll track chief-executive-era debt movements so the public can see what worked—and what didn’t. [oai_citation:15‡roderickjy.github.io](https://roderickjy.github.io/roderick-campaign-site/)
