Housing: Build Up, Not Bills

We need more homes without smashing current ratepayers. That means quality infill, mid-rise where infrastructure exists, and consents that move.

Unlock Supply Where Pipes Already Exist

  • Enable quality infill + mid-rise in serviced areas.
  • Fast-track near transport spines and town centres.
  • Design guides to keep neighbourhood character and sunlight.

Growth Pays Its Way

  • Clear, public development contribution calculator.
  • No hidden subsidies from existing ratepayers.
  • Targeted infrastructure agreements tied to delivery dates.

Consent Service Levels

  • Time-bound SLAs; weekly public dashboard.
  • “Tell us once” document checks; no ping-pong.
  • Case managers for complex projects so applicants have one accountable contact.

Safer, Better Streets

  • Hotspot lighting, CPTED upgrades, and enforcement partnerships.
  • Parks and footpaths maintained on visible schedules.
  • Monthly neighbourhood clinics in West Ward.

How to Vote (STV)

Write numbers, not ticks. #1 is your favourite, #2 your back-up, #3 still good. Keep ranking all candidates you’d be happy with — your vote keeps working if earlier choices are elected with surplus or eliminated.
